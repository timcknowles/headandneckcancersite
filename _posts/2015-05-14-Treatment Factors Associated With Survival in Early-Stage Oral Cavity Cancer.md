                    <strong>Primary endpoint(s)</strong>
                </p>
            </td>
            <td width="294" valign="top">
                <p>
                    5-year survival:
                </p>
                <p>
                    - Neck dissection, no radiotherapy, no chemotherapy, case
                    volume &gt;10/year and negative tumour margins all
                    beneficial (p=0.001 for all)
                </p>
                <p>
                    Overall survival:
                </p>
                <p>
                    - Neck dissection (p=0.003), no radiotherapy (p&lt;0.001),
                    no chemotherapy (p=0.03), academic institution (p=0.03) and
                    negative tumour margins (p=0.005) all beneficial
                </p>
            </td>
